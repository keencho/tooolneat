What are UTM Parameters?

UTM (Urchin Tracking Module) parameters are tags added to URLs that allow you to track marketing campaign effectiveness in Google Analytics.

Required Parameters

  • utm_source: Traffic source (google, facebook)
  • utm_medium: Marketing medium (cpc, email)
  • utm_campaign: Campaign name

Optional Parameters

  • utm_term: Paid search keywords
  • utm_content: Ad/link differentiation (A/B testing)

Best Practices

  • Use lowercase (Google Analytics is case-sensitive)
  • Use underscores (_) or hyphens (-) instead of spaces
  • Maintain consistent naming conventions (e.g., facebook vs fb)
  • Special characters are automatically encoded

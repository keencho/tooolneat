FAQ

Q: When does the game end?

The game ends when the snake's head hits its own body or the wall.

Q: How is the score calculated?

Each food item gives 10 points. Faster speeds make it harder but the score per food is the same.

Q: Is my high score saved?

Yes, it's saved in your browser's local storage and persists across visits.

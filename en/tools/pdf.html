Frequently Asked Questions

Are my PDF files uploaded to a server?

No. All PDF processing happens directly in your browser. Files are never uploaded to any server, keeping your privacy safe.

Is there a maximum file size limit?

It depends on your browser's memory, but we recommend PDFs under 50MB. Larger files may process more slowly.

Can I process password-protected PDFs?

Password-protected PDFs must be unlocked first. We currently don't support password removal functionality.

Which browsers are supported?

Works with all modern browsers including Chrome, Firefox, and Edge. Chrome is recommended for the best experience.

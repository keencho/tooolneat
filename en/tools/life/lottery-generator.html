About Lottery Number Generator

The Lottery Number Generator is a tool that randomly generates numbers for the Korean Lotto 6/45 game. It picks 6 unique winning numbers and 1 bonus number from 1 to 45, with no duplicates.

The generated numbers are selected with equal probability, just like a real lottery draw. Enjoy the fun animations as each number appears one by one!

How to Use

  1. Click the 'Generate Numbers' button
  2. Watch as 6 winning numbers and a bonus number appear with animations
  3. Generated numbers are automatically saved to history (up to 5 results)
  4. Toggle the sound button to turn sound effects on or off

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: What are the odds of winning with these numbers?

The odds of winning the lottery jackpot are approximately 1 in 8.14 million. Numbers generated by this tool have the same probability as any numbers you pick yourself. Every number combination has equal chances of winning.

Q: What do the ball colors mean?

These are the official colors used in Korean Lotto: 1-10 Yellow, 11-20 Blue, 21-30 Red, 31-40 Gray, 41-45 Green.

Q: What is the bonus number?

The bonus number is used to determine second prize winners. If you match 5 out of 6 main numbers plus the bonus number, you win second prize.

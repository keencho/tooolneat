What is an IP Address?

An IP (Internet Protocol) address is a unique numerical identifier assigned to every device connected to the internet. Your public IP address is visible on the internet, while private IP addresses are used within local networks.

Types of IP Addresses

  • IPv4: 32-bit address (e.g., 192.168.1.1)
  • IPv6: 128-bit address (e.g., 2001:db8::1)
  • Public IP: Unique address on the internet
  • Private IP: Local network address

Good to Know

  • Using a VPN may show a different IP address
  • Location data is approximate and may not be accurate
  • Your IP may change depending on your ISP

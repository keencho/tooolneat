What is a Tip Calculator?

A tip calculator is a convenient tool that helps you quickly and accurately calculate tips at restaurants, cafes, hotels, and other service establishments. It also automatically calculates the split amount when dining with multiple people.

Key Features

  1. Quick Tip Selection - Preset buttons for 10%, 15%, 18%, 20%, and 25% tips
  2. Custom Slider - Precisely adjust any tip percentage from 0% to 50%
  3. Bill Splitting - Enter the number of people to automatically calculate per-person amounts
  4. Round Up Option - Round the total to the nearest dollar for easier payments

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: How much should I tip?

Generally, 15-20% is standard. For exceptional service, tip 20-25%. For regular service, 15-18% is appropriate.

Q: Should I tip before or after tax?

Traditionally, tips are calculated on the pre-tax amount (subtotal), but calculating on the post-tax total is also acceptable.

Q: When should I tip more or less?

Tip more for exceptional service, large groups, or complex orders. You might tip less for poor service, but consider speaking to management about any issues.

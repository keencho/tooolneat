What is Aspect Ratio?

Aspect ratio represents the proportional relationship between the width and height of a screen. 16:9 is the most common widescreen ratio, used by most TVs and monitors.

Common Ratios

  • 16:9 - HD TV, YouTube, most monitors
  • 4:3 - Old TVs, iPad, presentations
  • 21:9 - Ultrawide monitors, cinema
  • 1:1 - Instagram square
  • 9:16 - Smartphone vertical, TikTok, Reels

Use Cases

  • Determining output resolution for video editing
  • Planning website image sizes
  • Comparing screen sizes when buying monitors/TVs
  • Designing responsive layouts

What is Spin the Wheel?

Spin the Wheel is a fun random selection tool. Use it when you need to pick one option randomly from multiple choices, like deciding what to eat for lunch or picking a winner.

How to Use

  1. Enter text in the input field and click 'Add' button
  2. Add at least 2 items to enable the 'SPIN' button
  3. Click 'SPIN' to spin the wheel
  4. When the wheel stops, the result will be displayed

FAQ

Q: How many items can I add?

You can add up to 12 items.

Q: Is the result truly random?

Yes, completely random. Each item has an equal probability of being selected.

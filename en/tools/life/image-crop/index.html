About Image Crop

Select and crop any part of your image. Perfect for creating social media profile pictures, thumbnails, or removing unwanted areas.

How to Use

  1. Drag & drop or click to upload an image
  2. Select an aspect ratio (Free, 1:1, 16:9, etc.)
  3. Drag the crop area to adjust position
  4. Drag corners or edges to resize
  5. Click "Crop & Download"

Aspect Ratio Guide

  • 1:1: Instagram posts, profile pictures
  • 4:3: Standard photos, presentations
  • 16:9: YouTube thumbnails, widescreen
  • 9:16: Instagram/TikTok Stories, Reels

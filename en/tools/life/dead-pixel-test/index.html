Dead Pixel Test

Fill your screen with solid colors to detect dead pixels, stuck pixels, or hot pixels. Click a color button below to enter fullscreen mode.

How to Use

  • • Click a color button → Enter fullscreen mode
  • • Press ← → or click to change colors
  • • Press ESC to exit
  • • Carefully scan the entire screen for any abnormal spots.

What are Dead Pixels?

Dead Pixel: A pixel that is always black. The backlight is blocked, so no light comes through.

Stuck Pixel: A pixel stuck on one color (red, green, or blue) that doesn't change.

Hot Pixel: A pixel that is always white or bright.

What is Color Picker?

Color Picker (EyeDropper) is a tool that allows you to extract colors from anywhere on your screen. Using the EyeDropper API, you can pick colors not only from web pages but also from other applications like Slack, Figma, Photoshop, and more.

How to Use

  1. Click the "Pick Color" button
  2. Move your cursor over any color on the screen
  3. Click to extract the color
  4. Copy the color in HEX, RGB, or HSL format

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Which browsers are supported?

Chrome 95+ and Edge 95+ are supported. Safari and Firefox do not yet support the EyeDropper API.

Q: Can I pick colors from outside the browser?

Yes! Once the eyedropper is activated, you can pick colors from anywhere on your screen, including other applications.

Q: The extracted color doesn't seem accurate

Color accuracy may vary slightly depending on your display color profile and browser rendering. For precise colors, check the original source's color code.

All processing is done in your browser. No data is sent to any server.

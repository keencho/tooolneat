What is Emoji Picker?

Emoji Picker is a tool for searching and copying emojis. Click an emoji to copy it to your clipboard, ready to paste into messages, documents, or social media.

How to Use

  1. Search by entering keywords (e.g., "smile", "heart", "fire")
  2. Or browse by category using the tabs above
  3. Click an emoji to copy it to your clipboard
  4. Paste anywhere with Ctrl+V (or Cmd+V on Mac)

FAQ

How do I copy an emoji?

Simply click on any emoji. It will be automatically copied to your clipboard.

Why don't some emojis display correctly?

Emoji display depends on your device and operating system. Newer emojis may not appear on older systems.

Are my recent emojis saved?

Yes, recently used emojis are saved in your browser's local storage for quick access.

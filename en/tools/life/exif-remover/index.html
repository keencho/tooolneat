What is EXIF Metadata?

EXIF (Exchangeable Image File Format) is metadata embedded in digital photos. It's automatically recorded when you take a picture with a camera or smartphone.

Information That May Be Included

  • GPS Location: Exact coordinates where the photo was taken
  • Date/Time: When the photo was captured
  • Camera Info: Device model and manufacturer
  • Camera Settings: Aperture, shutter speed, ISO
  • Thumbnail: Small preview image

Why Remove It?

  • Uploading photos to social media may expose your home location
  • Selling items online could leak personal information
  • Protect privacy when posting to blogs or websites

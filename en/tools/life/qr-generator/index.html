What is a QR Code Generator?

A QR code generator creates Quick Response codes that can store various types of data including URLs, text, contact information, WiFi credentials, and more. QR codes are two-dimensional barcodes that can be scanned by smartphones and other devices. They are widely used in marketing, payments, event tickets, product packaging, and contactless information sharing.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: What types of data can QR codes store?

QR codes can store various data types including website URLs, plain text, email addresses, phone numbers, SMS messages, WiFi network credentials, geographic locations, calendar events, and contact cards (vCards). The maximum capacity is about 3KB of data, or roughly 4,296 alphanumeric characters.

Q: What size should my QR code be for printing?

The minimum recommended size depends on scanning distance. For close-range scanning (within 10cm), a 2cm x 2cm QR code works well. For posters viewed from 1-2 meters, use at least 10cm x 10cm. A general rule is the scanning distance divided by 10 equals the minimum QR code size. Always test before mass printing.

Q: Can I customize the colors of my QR code?

Yes, QR codes can be customized with different colors, but ensure sufficient contrast for reliable scanning. The foreground should be darker than the background. Avoid low-contrast combinations and test your colored QR code with multiple devices before use. Traditional black on white provides the best scan reliability.

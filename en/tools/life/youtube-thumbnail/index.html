YouTube Thumbnail Downloader

Enter a YouTube video URL to download thumbnails in various resolutions.

Download Options

How to Download YouTube Thumbnails

  1. Copy the URL of the YouTube video you want
  2. Paste the URL in the input field above
  3. Click the search button to display thumbnails
  4. Click the download button for your desired resolution

Supported URL Formats

  • https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=VIDEO_ID
  • https://youtu.be/VIDEO_ID
  • https://youtube.com/shorts/VIDEO_ID

Resolution Guide

  • Max Resolution: 1280×720 (HD) - Highest quality
  • SD: 640×480 - Standard definition
  • HQ: 480×360 - High quality
  • MQ: 320×180 - Medium quality
  • Default: 120×90 - Default thumbnail

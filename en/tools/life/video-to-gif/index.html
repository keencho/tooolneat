Video to GIF Conversion

Convert video clips to GIF animations. Perfect for social media, blogs, and messaging.

How to Use

  1. Drag or click to upload a video file
  2. Adjust start/end time with sliders
  3. Select GIF width and FPS
  4. Click 'Convert to GIF' button

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Is there a length limit for GIFs?

Maximum 30 seconds. Longer GIFs result in very large files, so shorter is recommended.

Q: How should I set the FPS?

10 FPS is standard. 5 FPS makes smaller files, 15-24 FPS is smoother but larger.

Q: How can I reduce GIF file size?

Reduce width, lower FPS, and shorten duration to decrease file size.

Q: What video formats are supported?

MP4, WEBM, MOV and most formats that browsers can play are supported.

All processing is done in your browser. No data uploaded to servers.

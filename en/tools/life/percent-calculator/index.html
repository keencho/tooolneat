What is a Percentage Calculator?

A percentage calculator is a versatile tool that helps you perform various percentage-related calculations quickly and easily. Whether you need to find a percentage of a number, calculate discounts, or determine percentage changes, this calculator offers five different modes to handle all your percentage calculation needs.

Key Features

  1. X% of Y - Calculate what percentage of a number equals (e.g., What is 15% of 200?)
  2. X is what % of Y? - Find what percentage one number is of another (e.g., 30 is what % of 200?)
  3. % Change - Calculate the percentage increase or decrease between two values
  4. Adjust by % - Increase or decrease a value by a specific percentage
  5. Discount Calculator - Calculate sale prices and savings from discount percentages

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: What does percent mean?

Percent means "per hundred" or "out of 100." When you say 50%, it means 50 out of 100, which equals half (1/2). Similarly, 25% means a quarter (1/4) of the whole.

Q: How do I calculate percentage increase vs decrease?

An increase shows as a positive percentage (+), while a decrease shows as negative (-). For example, going from 100 to 120 is a +20% increase, while going from 100 to 80 is a -20% decrease.

Q: How do I calculate a discount?

To calculate a discount, multiply the original price by the discount percentage, then subtract from the original. For example, 30% off $100: $100 x 0.30 = $30 discount, final price = $70.

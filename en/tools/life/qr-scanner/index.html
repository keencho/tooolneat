What is QR Code Scanner?

A tool that reads information from QR codes. Recognizes URLs, text, contacts, Wi-Fi info and more.

How to Use

  1. Choose camera or image upload
  2. Camera: Point at QR code
  3. Image: Upload image with QR code
  4. View result and copy/open

FAQ

Q: Camera not working?

Allow camera permission in your browser. Only works on HTTPS.

Q: What QR codes are supported?

URLs, text, vCard, Wi-Fi, email and most QR code formats.

Q: Can it scan barcodes?

This is QR code only. Use Barcode Generator for barcodes.

All processing is done in your browser. No data uploaded to servers.

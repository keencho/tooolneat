Speech to Text

Speak into your microphone for real-time transcription. Perfect for meeting notes and dictation.

Browser Compatibility Notice

This feature works best in Chrome, Edge, and Safari. Firefox has limited support.

Click the microphone to start

You can edit the text directly

Browser-based processing - audio is not sent to our servers

About Speech Recognition

This tool uses the Web Speech API to convert your voice to text in real-time. It's perfect for taking meeting notes, transcribing interviews, or hands-free typing.

How to Use

  1. Select your language from the dropdown
  2. Click the microphone button
  3. Allow microphone access in your browser
  4. Start speaking - text appears in real-time
  5. Click again to stop recording

FAQ

Q: Which browsers are supported?

Chrome, Edge, and Safari work best. Firefox has limited Web Speech API support.

Q: Why is accuracy low?

Speak clearly in a quiet environment, close to your microphone. Using an external microphone can also improve results.

Q: Is my voice data sent to servers?

The Web Speech API uses your browser's built-in speech recognition. Depending on your browser, it may use cloud services, but no data is sent to our servers.

Q: Does it work offline?

Most browsers require an internet connection. Some devices support offline speech recognition.

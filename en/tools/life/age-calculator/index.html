What is an Age Calculator?

An age calculator determines your exact age based on your date of birth. It calculates your international age (exact age), Korean age, total days and hours lived, and shows your Western zodiac sign and Chinese zodiac animal.

Korean Age vs International Age

Korean Age (Counting Age): In the traditional Korean age system, a person is 1 year old at birth, and everyone gains one year on January 1st regardless of their actual birthday. For example, a baby born in December would be 1 year old at birth and turn 2 on January 1st of the following month.

International Age (Western Age): A person is 0 years old at birth and gains one year on each birthday. This is the system used in most countries around the world.

Since June 2023, South Korea officially uses international age for legal and administrative purposes.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: How is international age calculated?

International age is calculated by subtracting your birth date from today's date. If your birthday has passed this year, your age is (current year - birth year). If your birthday hasn't occurred yet, your age is (current year - birth year - 1).

Q: Why is Korean age higher?

Korean age starts at 1 at birth and increases by 1 every New Year's Day. Therefore, Korean age is typically 1-2 years higher than international age, depending on when your birthday falls in the year.

Q: How is the Chinese zodiac determined?

The Chinese zodiac is determined by your birth year in a 12-year cycle. The animals in order are: Rat, Ox, Tiger, Rabbit, Dragon, Snake, Horse, Goat, Monkey, Rooster, Dog, and Pig.

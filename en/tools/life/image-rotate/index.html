About Image Rotate/Flip

Fix photos taken in the wrong orientation on your smartphone, or create mirror images easily.

How to Use

  1. Drag & drop or click to upload an image
  2. Click the rotate/flip buttons as needed
  3. You can apply multiple transformations in sequence
  4. Click 'Download' to save your edited image

Features

  • Left 90°: Rotate counter-clockwise by 90 degrees
  • Right 90°: Rotate clockwise by 90 degrees
  • 180°: Rotate the image upside down
  • Flip Horizontal: Mirror the image left-to-right
  • Flip Vertical: Mirror the image top-to-bottom
